The Soul of Tirta Tawar

OUR
ESSENCE.

Starting from Unrest

Duue Coffee wasn't born in a luxury office building, but rather in a small garage in a corner of Ubud in early 2024. We saw specialty coffee slowly becoming something "cold" and full of intimidating regulations.
We want to return coffee to its roots: a medium for socializing. By restoring every corner of our own space, Duue Coffee was built as a place where the aroma of coffee meets the honest rhythm of Tirta Tawar's streets.
01

Our Name

The duality that merges. The name **Duue** is a reminder of the inseparable relationship between the farmer who plants with patience and us who brews it with precision. We believe that honest taste only emerges from fair collaboration. Without either, your cup is just plain water—With Duue, it becomes a story.

02

Color Spectrum

Our visual story is about harmony. The dark green we use represents the lushness of Ubud's forests that soothe the soul, while the golden yellow symbolizes the warmth of interaction at our tables. We designed Duue to be an "oasis" where you feel calm yet inspired by the energy within.

03

Service Etiquette

Here, you are not just a queue number. We implement an inclusive service culture—where coffee education is given without an intimidating impression. We serve people with all their stories, creating a community that grows together with every sip, ensuring every guest leaves with a smile and new inspiration.

ROOTED IN UBUD.
