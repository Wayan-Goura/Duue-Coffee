Starting from Unrest

Duue Coffee wasn't born in a luxury office building, but rather in a small garage in a corner of Ubud in early 2024. We saw specialty coffee slowly becoming something "cold" and full of intimidating regulations.

We want to return coffee to its roots: a medium for socializing. By restoring every corner of our own space, Duue Coffee was built as a place where the aroma of coffee meets the honest rhythm of Tirta Tawar's streets.

01

Our Name

The duality that merges. The name Duue is a reminder of the inseparable relationship between the farmer who plants with patience and us who brews it with precision. We believe that honest taste only emerges from fair collaboration.

02

Color Spectrum

Our visual story is about harmony. The dark green represents the lushness of Ubud's forests that soothe the soul, while the golden yellow symbolizes the warmth of interaction. An "oasis" designed to make you feel calm yet inspired.
